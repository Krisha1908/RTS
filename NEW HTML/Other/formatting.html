Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal was built between 1631 and 1648 by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal, who died during childbirth. The construction of the monument involved the labor of thousands of artisans, craftsmen, and laborers. The Taj Mahal is a renowned and iconic monument located in Agra, India. It is widely regarded as one of the most beautiful and exquisite examples of Mughal architecture and is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Great Pyramid of Giza

The Great Pyramid was built during the reign of Pharaoh Khufu (also known as Cheops), who was a ruler of the Fourth Dynasty of ancient Egypt. It is estimated to have been constructed around 2560 BC. The pyramid was initially intended to serve as a monumental tomb for the pharaoh. The Great Pyramid of Giza, also known as the Pyramid of Khufu or the Pyramid of Cheops, is one of the most iconic and enduring symbols of ancient Egyptian civilization. It is the largest and oldest of the three pyramids located on the Giza Plateau, near Cairo, Egypt

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a world-famous archaeological site located in the Andes Mountains of Peru. It is often referred to as the "Lost City of the Incas" and is renowned for its stunning beauty, well-preserved ruins, and the mystery surrounding its purpose and abandonment. Machu Picchu was "rediscovered" by American historian and explorer Hiram Bingham in 1911. While the site was not completely unknown to locals, Bingham's expedition brought international attention to this remarkable archaeological treasure.

colosseum

The Colosseum, also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is a historic and iconic ancient Roman structure located in the heart of Rome, Italy. It is one of the most famous and recognizable landmarks in the world, renowned for its grandeur, architectural ingenuity, and historical significance. The Colosseum was built during the Flavian dynasty, commissioned by Emperor Vespasian around AD 70-72 and completed in AD 80 by his successor and heir, Emperor Titus
